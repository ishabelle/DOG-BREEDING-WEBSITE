ABOUT US

For over 10 years I have been breeding Cane Corso Italiano dogs, associated in the Polish Kennel Club. Since I remember dogs occupied a special place in my life. Hitting this breed, my life turned upside down! From then on, I had one desire - to create a kennel out of love for dogs and the passion that develops every day. Each time I select genes carefully so that the next generations of dogs are worthy and healthy representatives of the breed. Strong, balanced dogs come out of my kennel and they are wonderful members of their new families.

Adriana Zukowska
